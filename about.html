About

I’m a freelance UX Designer and Front End Developer pursuing a full-time position. I live in Redondo Beach, located in the greater Los Angeles area.

I earned my Bachelor of Science degree in Food Science from Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. Transitioning into tech, I earned my Full Stack Web Development certificate from UCLA Extension. With this foundation in coding, I continued with education in User Experience Design. I earned the UI/UX Design Specialization Certificate from Cal Arts, in addition to UX-related certifications from other programs.

I studied Food Science with a desire to combine science and creativity in product development. In Research and Development Management and Quality Assurance, I took products from ideation to launch. My management background cultivated my comprehensive approach to problem solving and provided me with an aptitude for understanding the needs of clients and customers.

I love the outdoors, volleyball, pickleball, and learning about food and drink. I also enjoy photography, painting, and video games.
