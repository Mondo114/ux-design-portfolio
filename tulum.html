Cal Poly Tech Startup Weekend, 2nd Place

Tech Startup Weekend Synopsis

• Network and pitch business ideas

• Choose a project to work on with a group and decide on roles for each group member

• Work on business pitch presentation and company design for two days, then present your pitch in front of judges from professional backgrounds (entrepreneurs, designers, etc.)

In a nutshell

business pitch for company's product or service that solves a problem for users; our group won 2nd place for our business pitch and design

Our group's take

cosmetics line that covers up while also treating cold sores - makeup and medical treatment in one product; my role for the weekend was to design a website homepage and logo

Why

Tech Startup Weekend: network and work with other people in a fast-paced, creative environment

Who

group of three (groups were different sizes - up to 15 members)

Technologies

Sketch

Desktop Website

Tulum is a town located on the Caribbean coastline of Mexico’s Yucatán Peninsula. It has beautiful beaches and well-preserved ruins of an ancient Mayan port city. The website needed to be simple and straightforward, yet elegant and sophisticated. The dark grey, light blue and green colors, along with the typeface, help portray this.

Logo

For the logo, it was necessary to communicate that these products are a combination of cosmetic and medical. The colors of the flower petals with the negative space of a medical cross convey this. Further development of the branding would push for each flower petal having its own meaning - confidence, soothe, recover, and beauty.
