Food Delivery Website Project Brief:

Cal Arts Assignment: "Imagine that you've been hired by an entrepreneur who wants to open a new, delivery-only establishment that delivers food (or drinks) to customers living nearby. You have been asked to plan and design the restaurant’s website. The centerpiece of this website will be an online food customization and ordering system."

Project Ideas

Diabetic Delights - Aimed at senior citizens - San Luis Obispo - $$ - Meals targeted to diabetic senior citizens so they don’t have to worry about what they can and can’t have - Problem: Not super familiar with diabetic needs, would need to learn more Microgurt - Aimed at families - Los Angeles South Bay - $$ - Subscription-based, made-to-order yogurt deliver that uses high quality ingredients - Problem: Ensuring live cultures stay active

Research

Restaurant Name & URL Microgurt www.microgurt.com (not currently in actual use) Elevator pitch Microgurt is a subscription-based, made-to-order yogurt delivery company that uses high quality ingredients with a focus on providing a substantial quantity of probiotic microorganisms to establish and maintain a happy digestive system. Location Los Angeles South Bay Main target audience - Families - New parents Cost - Mid-level price: made in small batches with high-quality ingredients - About $6.00 for 1 qt - 1 qt is minimum purchase - Suggested subscription: 1 qt per person per week (about ½ cup eaten each day)
Food & drink Subscription-based, custom probiotic yogurt for delivery Base: Cream + Milk (regular) Skim Milk (low fat) Coconut Cream + Coconut Milk (non-dairy) + Add-Ins: Fruits Nuts Nut Butters Spices Honey Agave Nectar Molasses Vanilla Malt Powder Cocoa Powder + Probiotics (can make several add-in categories like “sweeteners”)
